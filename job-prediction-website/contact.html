Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our job prediction model

How accurate is the job prediction model?

+

Our job prediction model has an accuracy rate of approximately 85% based on our testing with historical data. However, accuracy can vary depending on the completeness of the information provided and current job market trends.

What data do you use for predictions?

+

Our model analyzes various factors including your education, work experience, skills, and interests. We also incorporate current job market trends and salary data to provide the most relevant career suggestions.

Is my personal information secure?

+

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I get career advice based on the results?

+

Absolutely! Our team of career experts can provide personalized advice based on your prediction results. Feel free to reach out through our contact form to schedule a consultation.
